# Zuru Web Solutions Website - Development Plan

## Design Guidelines

### Design References (Primary Inspiration)
- **Stripe.com**: Clean, professional, tech-forward aesthetic
- **Vercel.com**: Modern minimalism with sharp edges
- **Style**: Professional Tech + Blue-Silver Metallic + Sharp & Premium

### Color Palette
- Primary: #1E3A8A (Deep Blue - primary brand)
- Secondary: #3B82F6 (Bright Blue - accents)
- Metallic Silver: #C0C0C0 (Silver - highlights)
- Dark: #0F172A (Slate - backgrounds)
- Light: #F8FAFC (Off-white - sections)
- Text: #1E293B (Dark slate), #64748B (Gray - secondary)
- Accent: #60A5FA (Light blue - CTAs)

### Typography
- Heading1: Inter font-weight 800 (56px) - Sharp, bold headlines
- Heading2: Inter font-weight 700 (40px) - Section titles
- Heading3: Inter font-weight 600 (28px) - Subsection titles
- Body/Normal: Inter font-weight 400 (16px) - Readable body text
- Body/Emphasis: Inter font-weight 600 (16px) - Important text
- Navigation: Inter font-weight 500 (15px) - Clean navigation

### Key Component Styles
- **Buttons**: Blue gradient (from #3B82F6 to #1E3A8A), white text, 8px rounded, metallic shine effect on hover
- **Cards**: White background with subtle silver border, 12px rounded, shadow on hover with blue glow
- **Forms**: Clean white inputs with blue focus ring, sharp corners (6px rounded)
- **Navigation**: Sticky header with glass morphism effect, blue-tinted backdrop blur

### Layout & Spacing
- Hero section: Full viewport height with animated gradient background
- Content sections: Alternating white/light gray backgrounds
- Section padding: 100px vertical on desktop, 60px on mobile
- Card hover: Lift 4px with blue shadow glow, 300ms transition
- Sharp geometric shapes and angular design elements
